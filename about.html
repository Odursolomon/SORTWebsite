Solomon Odur Research Technologies (SORT) is a research, data analytics, monitoring & evaluation, GIS, and innovation consultancy dedicated to generating evidence, delivering insights, and developing practical solutions that create measurable impact.

Based in Uganda, SORT supports clients with research, data analytics, monitoring & evaluation, GIS intelligence, and innovation products that translate evidence into operational action.

Our work focuses on practical rigor: clear questions, strong methods, clean data, defensible analysis, and outputs that decision-makers can use.
