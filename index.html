SORT Flagship Product

FAESLIMS: Laboratory Management Platform

SORT has developed FAESLIMS, a comprehensive laboratory information management system designed for research institutions and diagnostic centers. FAESLIMS streamlines sample lifecycle management, documentation, reporting, inventory control, financial tracking, and compliance—all in one integrated platform.
