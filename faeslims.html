SORT Flagship Product

FAESLIMS: Laboratory Information Management for the Modern Research Lab

A comprehensive platform for managing sample lifecycle, documentation, reporting, inventory control, financial tracking, and compliance in research and clinical laboratories across Uganda and Africa.

Request Demo Contact SORT

What is FAESLIMS?

Laboratory operations made simple, transparent, and compliant.

FAESLIMS is a cloud-based laboratory information management system built by SORT to help research institutions, diagnostic centers, and clinical laboratories streamline operations. From sample intake to results reporting, FAESLIMS provides the clarity, structure, and documentation your laboratory needs to deliver reliable, defensible results.

Key Features

Everything your laboratory needs in one platform.

01

Sample Lifecycle Management

Track samples from receipt through testing, analysis, archiving, and disposal. Complete audit trail and chain of custody documentation.

02

Test & Workflow Automation

Define lab protocols and automate routine workflows. Route samples intelligently and reduce manual data entry.

03

Results & Reporting

Generate professional reports, quality-assured results, and batch exports. Integration with external systems for seamless data handoff.

04

Inventory & Equipment Management

Track reagents, supplies, equipment calibration, and maintenance. Automated alerts for expiry, stock levels, and service schedules.

05

Financial & Billing

Record test costs, generate invoices, and track revenue per sample or project. Cost control and financial reporting at your fingertips.

06

Compliance & Quality Assurance

Built-in support for ISO, CAP, and other laboratory standards. Document quality controls, deviations, and corrective actions effortlessly.

Built For Your Lab

Designed by researchers, for researchers and lab managers.

FAESLIMS was built from real laboratory challenges. SORT worked with lab teams across Uganda and East Africa to understand workflows, pain points, and the data your teams actually need to make decisions.

The result is a system that fits how labs really work—not how software vendors imagine they work.

Who Uses FAESLIMS

Built for diverse laboratory environments.

Research Institutions

Universities and research centers managing high-volume sample testing, multi-project tracking, and publication-ready data documentation.

Diagnostic Centers

Clinical and diagnostic labs processing samples daily, managing patient privacy, and delivering timely, accurate results.

Environmental & Quality Labs

Water quality, food testing, and environmental monitoring labs needing reliable data management and regulatory compliance.
